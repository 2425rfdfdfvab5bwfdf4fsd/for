OBJECTIVE:
    Implement VolatilityFilter that blocks scanning when ATR indicates
    abnormally low or abnormally high volatility conditions.

CONTEXT:
    Normal trading requires adequate volatility to reach TP and avoid noise.
    Extremes in either direction increase risk:
    - Too low ATR: spread consumes too much of the expected move
    - Too high ATR: price moves violently, SL at normal distance easily hit

    ATR source: H1 ATR(14) calculated by strategy engine.

    Thresholds (configurable per symbol):
        MIN_ATR_PIPS: minimum acceptable ATR in pips (default: 5)
        MAX_ATR_PIPS: maximum acceptable ATR in pips (default: 80)

DEPENDENCIES:
    - Phase 05 Strategy Engine: ATR values computed
    - Phase 02 config

FILES TO CREATE:

TEST REQUIREMENTS:
