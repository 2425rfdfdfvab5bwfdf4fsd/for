OBJECTIVE:
    Implement RejectionJournal that records every signal or trade candidate
    that was rejected, along with the reason and score.

CONTEXT:
    Every rejection is valuable data. By logging rejections, we can:
    - Tune confluence thresholds
    - Identify the most common rejection reasons
    - Review near-misses (scored 7-7.9) to see if threshold is appropriate

    REJECTION CATEGORIES:
        CONFLUENCE_TOO_LOW      — score < 8
        DAILY_LIMIT_REACHED     — trade count or loss limit
        CONSECUTIVE_LOSS_BLOCK  — 2 consecutive losses
        CORRELATION_BLOCK       — correlated position open
        RR_INSUFFICIENT         — R:R < 2
        SESSION_BLOCKED         — outside trading session
        SPREAD_TOO_WIDE         — spread exceeded
        NEWS_BLACKOUT           — high-impact news
        FILTER_BLOCKED          — generic filter rejection
        DUPLICATE_SIGNAL        — signal deduplication
        EXECUTION_FAILED        — order send failed
