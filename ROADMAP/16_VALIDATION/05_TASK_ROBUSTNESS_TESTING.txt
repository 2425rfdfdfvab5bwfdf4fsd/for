OBJECTIVE:
    Implement RobustnessTester that subjects the backtest to stress conditions:
    higher spread, higher slippage, and different market regimes to ensure
    the strategy does not catastrophically fail under adverse conditions.

CONTEXT:
    ROBUSTNESS TESTS:
    1. 2× spread: double all spread costs
    2. 5× slippage: multiply slippage by 5
    3. Bull market period only (2020–2021)
    4. Bear market period only (2022)
    5. High volatility period only
    6. Low volatility period only

    Strategy should remain profitable (or at worst near breakeven) under
    2× spread and 5× slippage. If it goes deeply negative: NOT robust.

DEPENDENCIES:
    - Phase 15 BacktestEngine, RealisticExecutionSimulator

FILES TO CREATE:
    validation/robustness_testing.py
    results/robustness/

FILES FORBIDDEN TO MODIFY:
    app/strategy/
    ROADMAP/

DETAILED REQUIREMENTS:
    class RobustnessTester:
        run_all_tests(base_config) -> RobustnessReport

    RobustnessReport:
        test_results: list[RobustnessTestResult]
        overall_verdict: str   # "ROBUST", "FRAGILE", "ACCEPTABLE"

    RobustnessTestResult:
        test_name: str
        base_pnl: float
        stressed_pnl: float
        degradation_pct: float
        passed: bool   # degradation < 50% = passed
