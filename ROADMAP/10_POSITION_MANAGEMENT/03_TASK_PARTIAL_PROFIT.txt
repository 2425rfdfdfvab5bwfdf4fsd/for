PHASE:       10 — Position Management

OBJECTIVE:
    Implement PartialProfitManager that closes 50% of the position when price
    reaches TP1, locking in partial profit and reducing risk.

CONTEXT:
    PARTIAL PROFIT RULE:
    - Trigger: price reaches TP1 level
    - Action: close 50% of open lot size (PARTIAL_CLOSE_PERCENT default: 50)
    - Remaining 50% runs to TP2 with BE SL applied (handled by BreakEvenManager)
    - Partial close uses MT5 TRADE_ACTION_DEAL with reduced volume

    ENABLE_PARTIAL_PROFIT=true (default, configurable)

    NOTE: Only one partial close per trade. Once fired, do not re-trigger.

DEPENDENCIES:
    - 10-01 PositionManager
    - 10-02 BreakEvenManager (BE applied after partial close)
    - Phase 03 MT5

FILES TO CREATE:
    app/management/partial_profit.py
    tests/test_position_management/test_partial_profit.py

FILES FORBIDDEN TO MODIFY:
    app/mt5/
    app/risk/
    app/strategy/
    ROADMAP/

DETAILED REQUIREMENTS:
    class PartialProfitManager:
        check_and_apply(
            position: Position,
            trade_record: TradeRecord,
            current_price: float
        ) -> PartialCloseAction | None

    PartialCloseAction:
        close_lots: float      # 50% of original, rounded to lot step
        remaining_lots: float
        reason: str
        executed: bool

    GUARDS:
        - partial_already_taken flag in TradeRecord (prevent double-close)
        - close_lots rounded DOWN to lot step
        - close_lots >= min_lot (else skip partial close)

TEST REQUIREMENTS:
    - test_partial_triggered_at_tp1()
    - test_correct_partial_lot_calculation()
    - test_partial_not_triggered_twice()
    - test_partial_skipped_if_below_min_lot()

ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA:
    [ ] 50% closed at TP1
    [ ] Only fires once per trade
    [ ] All tests pass
