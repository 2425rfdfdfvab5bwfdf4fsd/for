================================================================================
PROJECT OBJECTIVE
================================================================================

Build a production-grade, fully automated MT5 Forex trading system that:
  - Runs locally on Windows 10/11
  - Connects directly to a locally installed MetaTrader 5 terminal
  - Uses Python (no LLM APIs, no paid APIs)
  - Executes 0–3 high-quality trades per day (never forced)
  - Targets approximately 55–65% win rate (NOT guaranteed — performance target only)
  - Prioritizes positive expectancy, controlled drawdown, profit factor, robustness
  - Requires $0 software/API cost
