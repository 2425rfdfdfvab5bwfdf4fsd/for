OBJECTIVE:
    Implement MetricsCalculator that computes all standard trading performance
    metrics from a list of simulated trades and an equity curve.

CONTEXT:
    Minimum required sample size: 30 trades per symbol (warn if below this).
    Note: Do NOT claim 55–65% win rate is achieved unless sample is statistically
    significant and results are from out-of-sample data.

DEPENDENCIES:
    - 15-02 BacktestEngine (produces BacktestResult)

FILES TO CREATE:
    backtesting/metrics.py
    tests/test_backtesting/test_metrics.py

FILES FORBIDDEN TO MODIFY:
    app/strategy/
    ROADMAP/

METRICS TO CALCULATE:

    TRADE STATISTICS:
        total_trades, winning_trades, losing_trades, breakeven_trades
        win_rate_pct, loss_rate_pct
        avg_win, avg_loss, largest_win, largest_loss
        avg_r_multiple, avg_duration_bars

    FINANCIAL:
        total_pnl, total_return_pct
        profit_factor = gross_profit / abs(gross_loss)
        expected_value = (win_rate * avg_win) + (loss_rate * avg_loss)
        max_drawdown_pct, max_drawdown_duration_bars
        recovery_factor = total_pnl / max_drawdown

    RISK-ADJUSTED:
        sharpe_ratio (annualized, risk-free=0)
        sortino_ratio
        calmar_ratio = annual_return / max_drawdown

    CONSISTENCY:
        consecutive_wins_max, consecutive_losses_max
        monthly_win_rate (pct of profitable months)

    STATISTICAL WARNINGS:
        low_sample_warning: bool   (< 30 trades)
        statistical_significance: str   ("LOW", "MODERATE", "HIGH")

TEST REQUIREMENTS:
    - test_win_rate_calculation()
    - test_profit_factor_calculation()
    - test_max_drawdown_calculation()
    - test_sharpe_ratio_calculation()
    - test_low_sample_warning_triggered()
