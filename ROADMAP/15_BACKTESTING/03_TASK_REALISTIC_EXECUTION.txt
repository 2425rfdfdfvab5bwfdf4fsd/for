PHASE:       15 — Backtesting

OBJECTIVE:
    Implement RealisticExecutionSimulator that models the cost and slippage
    of execution in the backtest, preventing overly optimistic results.

CONTEXT:
    EXECUTION MODEL:
    - Entry: open price of the next bar after signal (no fill at exact signal bar)
    - Slippage: random uniform in [0, BACKTEST_SLIPPAGE_PIPS] added to entry
                Direction: adds to buy entry price, subtracts from sell entry price
    - Spread: applied to all entries (entry = mid + BACKTEST_SPREAD_PIPS/2 for direction)
              Cost is on entry only — modeled as worsened fill price
    - Commission: BACKTEST_COMMISSION_PER_LOT in account currency (default: $7.00/lot)
                  Applied per trade at entry as negative cash adjustment
                  Round-trip: commission applied ONCE at entry (not again at exit)
    - Overnight swap: BACKTEST_OVERNIGHT_SWAP_LONG / BACKTEST_OVERNIGHT_SWAP_SHORT
                  Applied once per calendar day the position is open past market close
                  (Use broker's actual swap table for realistic backtest accuracy)
    - Partial fill: not simulated (assume full fill — FOK behavior)

    FORMULA:
      gross_pnl = (exit_price - entry_price) * direction * lots * pip_value
      commission_cost = lots * BACKTEST_COMMISSION_PER_LOT
      swap_cost = hold_days * lots * swap_rate_per_lot
      net_pnl = gross_pnl - commission_cost - swap_cost

    These costs are subtracted from all trade P&L in backtest.

    IMPORTANT: Using $7/lot commission without validating against your broker's
    actual commission table will make backtest P&L unrealistic. Update this value
    to match your broker before running production backtests.
